8 to CREATE

logo and postcard design

Logo design for 8 to CREATE, a Registered Student Organization at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. 8 to CREATE is a collaborative art exhibit in which 8 artists each create a largescale piece of art during an 8 hour period. Myself and a group of students at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign organized and held 8 to CREATE this past March. I designed the logo and the following brand materials to advertise for the show.

8 to CREATE’s mission is to present art, not only as a final product that is tangible, sold and bought; but also as a process, in which the journey of creation engages thought. In the 8 to CREATE exhibit, no artwork is sold, so there is no assigned value to the product. What remains in the artist and the public viewers is the memory and the experience of the process of creation.
