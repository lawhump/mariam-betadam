8 to CREATE

logo and postcard design

Logo design for 8 to CREATE, a Registered Student Organization at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. 8 to CREATE is a collaborative art exhibit in which 8 artists each create a largescale piece of art during an 8 hour period.

8 to CREATE’s mission is to present art, not only as a final product that is tangible, sold and bought; but also as a process, in which the journey of creation engages thought. In the 8 to CREATE exhibit, no artwork is sold, so there is no assigned value to the product. What remains in the artist and the public viewers is the memory and the experience of the process of creation.

Logo Form

8 artists have 8 hours to create 8 drawing; the goal for this logo was to convey this slogan.

Credit goes to Karolina Malacynska for coming up with the idea to combine the “8” and hourglass. In order to minimize the complexity of this logo I used the negative space of the 8 to create an hourglass.

Quad Day Cards

We handed out these cards at Quad Day to advertise for 8 to CREATE and find Freshman interested in joining the club. Quad Day is an annual event in which Freshman can learn about and sign up to join clubs/sports at the University.
