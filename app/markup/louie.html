Louie

book design

Louie is a rental company imagined for a class project by myself and two fellow Graphic Design students at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign: Dan Harmon (Image logo design) and Vicki Ortiz (Text logo design). The company allows college students to rent out items (from beds to blenders) temporarily (from a weekend to a whole academic year) from a local store. By doing so, we can offer students the ability to transform their campus apartments from merely a living space to a home.  

The photographs show the process a student would take when renting items from Louie. She receives a coffee grinder in a Louie Box. The box is easily recognizable as a Louie box. Inside the top flap of the box is a pre-made return slip sticker. This box makes receiving and returning rental items a headache free experience.
