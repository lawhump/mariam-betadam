Multiplex Monograph

book design

This book was designed for Jess Kiel-Wornson, an artist and graduate student at the University of Illinois. All artwork displayed in this book copyright Jess Kiel-Wornson. Click  here  to view the entire publication.
Based in the concepts of memory, home and space, this book was designed to showcase Ms. Wornson’s work alongside these ideas. The book is broken up into various chapters or places Ms. Wornson has lived and worked. The work she made in each location changes depending on the qualities of the space. Because her work relies heavily on the concept of memory, there are moments of déjà vu in this book; images that were previously seen are scattered in later chapters, distorted.
