Cahoot

app design

A twist between a social media and location finding app, Cahoot aims to encourage face to face interaction between close friends. Features include: viewing a real time map of your friends, sending and receiving same day events, viewing your friends' profiles, and group messaging.
Cahoot is an app designed by myself, Andrew Haglund (Industrial Design BFA from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign), and Robert Marohn (Graphic Design BFA student, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign). Robert Marohn also designed the Cahoot logo. Rights to Neutral Design Studio and client Muzammil Ali.
