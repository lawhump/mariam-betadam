Mehu

UI design

When Ray Ansong, a local salon owner, came to Neutral Design Studio he wanted to create an app for his business. The goal was to increase discoverability and booking for stylists and salons. Clients can use the app to find stylists near them and “stand in line” or book an appointment from the comfort of their own home.
We created an app that accommodates both the client and the stylist through differing interfaces. This app aims to connect clients with stylists, so we named it Mehu which means “to bring together” in our clients Ghanian language. This app was designed by myself (Branding and Visual) and Andrew Haglund (User Experience).

Browse Stylists

Find stylists in your area according to your needs, and quickly see photos, location, ratings, and availability.

Walk-In

Wait in line in the comfort of your own home while receiving live updates. For future appointments, book a spot on a stylist’s schedule.

For Stylists

Add photos and edit your profile, view your queue, and manage your schedule.

For Stylists

Receive updates on current and upcoming sessions so you can stay organized and on schedule.
