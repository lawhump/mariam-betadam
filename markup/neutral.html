Neutral Cycle

t-shirt design

Neutral Cycle is a bike repair shop and retail store in Champaign, Illinois. The project task was to create Tshirts customers would be proud to wear as well as something that embodies the shop’s mission: “We believe in the positive impact of the bicycle and in the freedom and simplicity it embodies. Bicycles are beautiful and functional, express us as riders and people, and take us wherever we go. Together we move the world.”

With this, I began brainstorming.

The t-shirts are currently sold at Neutral Cycle.
