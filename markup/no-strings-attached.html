No Strings Attached

poster design

These materials were designed for an a cappella group at the University of Illinois called No Strings Attached. No Strings Attached asked for a poster and ticket designed around their concert titled “Generations of Jazz”.

This poster was inspired by the improvisation of Jazz and design guidelines of Bauhaus. Much like Laszlo Moholy-Nagy design’s, the content was typeset unconventionally. Although Bauhaus emphasized simple, strong colors like red black and white, I used green, yellow and orange because the show took place in spring.
